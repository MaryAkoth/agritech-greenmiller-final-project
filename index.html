Welcome to Agritech Green Application

-Agritech Green is a revolutionary platform that empowers small-scale farmers by connecting them directly to schools for the sale of maize, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices.

-Our mission is to support farmers in enhancing their livelihoods while contributing to Kenya's food security and sustainability goals.

-By providing schools with locally sourced maize, we help reduce the country's dependence on imports, ensuring that more Kenyan-grown produce is utilized. Through this platform, we aim to bridge the gap between farmers and educational institutions, making the procurement process smoother, more transparent, and cost-effective.

-Whether you are a farmer ready to sell your maize or a school looking to procure quality produce for your feeding programs, Agritech Greenmillers is here to make the connection simple, secure, and beneficial for all.
